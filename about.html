Our Story

Founded in 1998, MSQ Enterprise has been at the forefront of plastic manufacturing in Pakistan for over two decades. What started as a small family business has grown into one of the most trusted names in the packaging industry.

Our journey began with a simple vision: to provide high-quality, affordable plastic packaging solutions to local businesses. Today, we serve clients across multiple industries including pharmaceuticals, textiles, food & beverage, and consumer goods.

With state-of-the-art manufacturing facilities and a commitment to innovation, we continue to push the boundaries of what's possible in plastic packaging while maintaining our core values of quality, integrity, and customer satisfaction.

Our Vision

To be the leading plastic manufacturing company in the region, recognized for innovation, quality, and sustainable practices while creating value for our customers, employees, and stakeholders.

Our Mission

To deliver superior plastic packaging solutions through continuous innovation, cutting-edge technology, and unwavering commitment to quality that exceeds customer expectations and contributes to a sustainable future.
